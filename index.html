Our Story

Where Tradition Meets the Turning Belt

Born from a love of Japanese culture and the joy of communal eating, Kaiten Sushi brings the authentic kaiten experience to your neighbourhood. Every plate that passes your seat was freshly made minutes ago.

Our chefs craft each piece by hand — from classic nigiri and maki to seasonal specialties — and send them around the table so you can eat at your own pace, at your own pleasure.
